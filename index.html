About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Cybersecurity, College of Engineering and Information Technology, Buraydah Private Colleges, Saudi Arabia. With a PhD in Optimizing ML for Cybersecurity from Zagazig University, Egypt, my research focuses on the intersection of Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Cybersecurity, specifically in IoT environments. I am passionate about advancing AI technologies, including Large Language Models (LLMs) and Generative AI, to solve complex real-world problems.
